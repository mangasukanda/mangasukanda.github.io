Reginae Carter &amp; her baby sister flaunt their smiles with adorable dimples in recent photo

Publish date: 2024-05-29

The American rapper's daughter has a baby sister from her mother's new relationship, and the sweet little girl's photo has already done the rounds in the media a few times since her birth.

Reginae Carter, the 19-year-old daughter of Lil Wayne and Toya Wright, is already pretty famous thanks to her connection to the famous rapper. Since 2017, she has also made a number of appearances on Growing Up Hip Hop: Atlanta.

Toya Wright welcomed a baby girl with Robert Rushing in February of 2018. And Carter absolutely dotes on her baby sister, regularly sharing photos with the 5-month-old little girl.

A recent image she shared showed the precious tot sitting on the table in front of her as she held her upright in a sitting position, smiling from over the baby's shoulder.

The sisters appeared to be sitting at a dining room table at home, with Carter wearing a burnt orange jacket with white accents.

The baby girl, who has been named Reign Ryan Rushing, wore a khaki green onesie with ruffled floral sleeves, and an adorable baby pink bow tied around her head. Both sisters were smiling, although Reign seemed unsure of where the camera was.

In the background, behind a sofa, a play ring presumably belonging to Reign could be seen.

"Hi stinka," Carter captioned the beautiful photo, with two red heart emojis.

Another image Carter shared that showed her little sister also included their mother. The three appeared to be all dressed up for a day out in the image.

Wright wore a pair of high waisted jeans, a white tank top, and a long cardigan with a floral pattern across the white fabric. She also carried a wooden bag, andd left her long black hair loose.

Carter wore a matching shorts and crop top outfit with a blue and white pattern across it, and a frill hanging off the bottom of both. She alos had on a pair of pink sunglasses, and carried her baby sister on her hip.

The tot had traded her pink bow for a blue one on this occasion, and had a bib on over her blue and white outfit.

Just two years ago two of Wright's brothers were found shot dead in a car. Carter was just as devastated at the loss of her uncles as her mother was, and paid tribute to them on the anniversary of their deaths.

"We miss you guys . So much . Today makes 2 Years since I heard Rudy laugh or seen josh smile . It’s hard losing someone who means so much to you but it gives me peace in my heart knowing you guys are in a better place . I love y’all so much. #justice4rudyandjosh," she wrote in the caption of the throwback photo.

Wright shared a tribute post of her own on the anniversary, writing:

“God grant me the serenity to accept the things I cannot change,the courage to change the things i can and the wisdom to know the difference. #itsbeentwoyears #JusticeforRudyandJosh #imissmybrothers #rudyandjoshforever.”
