Lauren London Shows off Her Stunning Waist-Length Hair Modeling the Latest Puma Collection

Publish date: 2024-06-21

Actress Lauren London showed off her beautiful waist-length dark mane as she modeled an attire from Puma's latest collection while squatting and flexing in a brand new photo.

Take a cue from Lauren London to style athleisure wear because she knows how to do it right! The beautiful 35-year-old blew the netizens' minds with a photo of herself clad in Puma from head to toe.

London carried herself like a queen as she posed in a black and white Puma tracksuit. The outfit was nothing extraordinary in itself, but the way she donned it with class made all the difference.

Nipsey Hussle and Lauren London attend a basketball game at Staples Center on November 14, 2018 in Los Angeles, California. | Source: Getty Images

LONDON STYLED IT LIKE A TRUE QUEEN

For footwear, she went with a matching pair of black and white sneakers from the athletic brand. Her laces were undone as she squatted on the street. Bright blue eyeshadow illuminated her eyes.

London continues to honor Hussle's legacy.

London let her long dark mane loose. The waist-length hair flowed down her shoulders towards her thighs as she posed for the camera. Fans loved her look and the comment section was alive with compliments.

Some of the fans shared that they could see the pain in her eyes, referring to the hurt caused by her boyfriend Nipsey Hussle's death. Last year, she and Puma shared that they would be collaborating on the Forever Stronger campaign.

A TRIBUTE TO LATE NIPSEY HUSSLE

The campaign kicked off with a heartwarming tribute to the late rapper, an ambassador for the brand. Back in January, London debuted the Hussle & Motivate collection which was released on February 1.

It has been over a year since Hussle was murdered in Los Angeles. He was shot multiple times in the parking lot of his Marathon Clothing store. The suspect, Eric Holder, was believed to be a friend of the rapper and the reason behind the shooting was deemed personal.

London continues to honor Hussle's legacy. On March 31, she paid an emotional tribute to him on the first anniversary of his death, saying she would give anything to see him again.

HUSSLE'S LEGACY

The late rapper is survived by London and their son Kross, who turned four in September. His birthday came two weeks after what would have been Hussle's 35th birthday.

Kross is his father's mini-me and is growing up to look like him. London is also the mother to her 10-year-old son Kameron Samuel Ari who she shares with her ex-fiancé Lil Wayne.

Hussle, who was merely 33 when he was murdered, posthumously bagged a Grammy this year in the best rap/sung performance category. He might be gone, but his loved ones are keeping his legacy alive.
