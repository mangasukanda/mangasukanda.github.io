Axel

Axel is a playable character in the Twisted Metal series. Known for his distinctive appearance and powerful abilities, Axel has become an iconic figure in the game.

Trapped between two gigantic tires by his father, Axel’s physical form sets him apart from the other characters. Despite his lack of speed, Axel makes up for it with devastating attacks and unique skills.

“With a mighty shock wave, Axel can send opponents flying and cause massive damage. His rooftop battles and ramming techniques make him a force to be reckoned with.”

While other characters may rely on speed and agility, Axel thrives in close-quarters combat and excels in strategic maneuvering. His ability to navigate small spaces and deliver powerful blows makes him a formidable foe on the battlefield.

Ready to unleash chaos and destruction as Axel? Brace yourself for an adrenaline-fueled experience like no other in the Twisted Metal universe.

Calypso

Calypso is the enigmatic creator of the Twisted Metal tournament. With the power to grant wishes, Calypso holds the fate of the winners in his hands. He is known for his cunning and manipulative nature, often using the desires of the contestants against them. Calypso’s voice is provided by Mel McMurrin, who brings the character to life with his unique portrayal. As one of the central figures in the Twisted Metal series, Calypso adds an element of mystery and intrigue to the game.

Calypso, with his mysterious persona and manipulative tactics, plays a crucial role in the Twisted Metal universe. Whether you find yourself drawn to his enigmatic nature or wary of his intentions, there’s no denying the impact Calypso has on the game.

Dollface

Dollface is a driver in the Twisted Metal series who wears a doll mask permanently attached to her face. Her haunting appearance and mysterious backstory make her a memorable and eerie character in the game.

Behind the doll mask, Dollface hides her true identity, adding to the air of mystery surrounding her. As players take control of Dollface, they enter a world where fear and power intertwine.

To match her haunting persona, Dollface drives the formidable vehicle known as Darkside. With its menacing presence and powerful capabilities, Darkside adds an element of horror to the Twisted Metal universe.

Players who choose Dollface will experience a unique combination of fear and power as they navigate the twisted battlegrounds and unleash havoc upon their opponents.

Embrace the darkness and step into the shoes of Dollface, the embodiment of fear and mystery in the Twisted Metal world.

Mr. Grimm

Mr. Grimm is a character that embodies darkness and death in the Twisted Metal series. With his motorcycle and skull helmet, he strikes fear into the hearts of his opponents. Known for his speed and agility, Mr. Grimm is a force to be reckoned with on the battlefield.

Players are drawn to Mr. Grimm’s mysterious nature and powerful attacks, making him a fan-favorite character in the Twisted Metal franchise. Whether you’re navigating through twisted streets or engaging in intense combat, Mr. Grimm’s presence is felt throughout the game.

Explore the underworld of Twisted Metal with Mr. Grimm and experience the thrill of embodying the Grim Reaper himself.

Sweet Tooth

Sweet Tooth is perhaps the most iconic character in the Twisted Metal series. This homicidal harlequin has been the face of the game since its inception. With his clown mask and flaming hair, Sweet Tooth strikes fear into the hearts of his opponents. He drives an ice cream truck that is equipped with deadly weapons, making him a force to be reckoned with in the game. Sweet Tooth’s unique design and chilling persona have made him a fan-favorite character and a staple of the Twisted Metal franchise.

As the embodiment of twisted evil and chaos, Sweet Tooth has become synonymous with the Twisted Metal series. His demented nature, combined with his ruthless gameplay style, has solidified his position as a top choice for gamers who crave a truly wicked experience. Whether you’re a fan of his macabre design, his powerful attacks, or the psychological terror he instills, Sweet Tooth is a character that truly embodies the essence of Twisted Metal.

Minion

Minion is a demon character that appears in multiple Twisted Metal games. He is recognizable by his large tank-like vehicle and imposing presence. Minion’s role in the series varies, but he is often portrayed as a powerful enemy that players must defeat. His demonic appearance and devastating attacks make him a formidable opponent in the world of Twisted Metal. Whether you’re facing off against Minion or playing as him, this character adds an element of darkness and supernatural power to the game.

Grasshopper

Grasshopper is a unique character in the Twisted Metal series, known for its unconventional design and nimble nature. This tiny modified go-cart/dune buggy may not boast the same level of armor or speed as other characters, but it more than makes up for it with its agility and surprise attacks. Grasshopper’s strategy revolves around catching opponents off guard and delivering devastating body slams with its special move.

Choosing Grasshopper as your character in Twisted Metal offers a challenging yet rewarding gameplay experience. Navigating the treacherous world of Twisted Metal becomes an art as you maneuver through tight spaces and dodge incoming attacks. With its small size and quick movements, Grasshopper can evade larger opponents and strike with precision. It takes a skilled player to master the art of Grasshopper and emerge victorious in the intense car combat battles.

Whether you’re looking for a different playstyle or simply want to try something new, Grasshopper provides a refreshing change of pace in the Twisted Metal franchise. Its unique abilities and never-back-down attitude make it a standout character among the roster.
