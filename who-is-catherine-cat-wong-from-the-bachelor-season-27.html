Who Is Catherine Cat Wong From The Bachelor Season 27?

Publish date: 2024-06-18

The Bachelor’s 27th season will premiere with a new eligible bachelor and his 30 suitors. Zach Shallcross, who previously participated in The Bachelorette season 19, will feature in the program. Catherine Cat Wong, a 26-year-old New York dancer, is one of Zach’s suitors in the forthcoming season. Cat is a free-spirited optimist who feels that true love is within her grasp. Season 27 of The Bachelor will begin on ABC on Monday, January 23, at 8 p.m. ET.

Meet Catherine Cat Wong before she appears on The Bachelor.

The Bachelor season 27 will feature 30 women competing to woo Zach Shallcross. Catherine Cat Wong, a 26-year-old dancer from New York City, will be one of the contestants. Cat adores dancing and picnics, and her perfect date consists of cheese and crackers in Central Park. The New Yorker in her enjoys hotdogs and despises dinner dates. In the future, she plans to cruise across the globe while following her dream of becoming a professional dancer. In high school, she was a sailing teacher.

Cat now has 4,479 Instagram followers, which is expected to grow after the episode airs. She often posts on social networking sites about her dance choreographies, her life in New York, and other topics. She routinely shares pictures of herself having fun with pals and adds shots from her trips to Miami and Las Vegas. Her highlights show that the Cats were involved behind the scenes at both the 2018 NFL Draft in Dallas and the NBA All-Star weekend in Charlotte, North Carolina, the same year.

According to her ABC bio:

“Cat likes living in New York City, dancing, and having a picnic in the park. Cat and cheese and crackers in Central Park? To us, this seems like a lovely one-on-one date! Cat is eager to meet someone who accepts her for who she is and would prioritize her. Cat feels that true love is within her grasp, and we hope she is correct.”

Cat is one of five individuals from The Bachelor season 27 who previously knew Zach. While watching The Bachelorette: After the Final Rose, Cat, Brianna, Bailey, Christina, and Brooklyn met their future cast mates and prospective love partners. Aly, Anastasia, Ariel, Bailey, Becca, Brianna, Brooklyn, Cara, Catherine, Charity, Christina, Davia, Gabriella, Genevie, Greer, Holland, Jess, Kaitlyn, Katherine, Kimberly, Kylee, Lekha Ravi, Madison, Mercedes, Olivia L, Olivia M, Sonia, Vanessa, Victoria J., and Viktoria E. will join them on the ABC show.

Get to know Zach Shallcross.

Zach Shallcross, 26, is a technology executive from Anaheim Hills, California. The family guy wishes to find love like his parents did. They’ve been married for 30 years and have shown him the meaning of genuine love.

Zach was previously featured on Gabby and Rachel’s season of The Bachelorette, when he developed a strong relationship with Rachel. However, he self-eliminated from the program because he believed she wasn’t the one for him since his age made her unclear whether he was ready for marriage. Watch ABC on Monday, January 23 at 8 p.m. ET to discover what happens as Season 27 debuts.
