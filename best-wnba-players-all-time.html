Top WNBA Players of All Time Ranked

Publish date: 2024-05-17

Who are the best WNBA players of all time? The debate has raged on for years, with fans and experts alike putting forward their own opinions. But now, a panel of experts has ranked the top WNBA players based on their on-court performance. These rankings include both past greats and currently active players, giving us a glimpse into the incredible talent that has shaped the WNBA throughout its history.

Key Takeaways:

  • Diana Taurasi is widely regarded as the greatest WNBA player of all time due to her consistent offensive greatness and longevity.
  • Tamika Catchings is recognized as one of the most complete basketball players in WNBA history, excelling in scoring, rebounding, defense, and leadership.

Diana Taurasi – The GOAT of the WNBA

Diana Taurasi, a guard for the Phoenix Mercury, is widely regarded as the greatest WNBA player of all time. With three championships, numerous accolades, and the highest offensive win share total in WNBA history, Taurasi’s consistent offensive greatness and longevity set her apart from her peers. She is a dominant scorer and a clutch performer, making her the clear choice for the top spot.

When it comes to determining the best WNBA player of all time, the name Diana Taurasi stands above the rest. Known for her deadly shooting, impeccable ball-handling, and unparalleled competitiveness, Taurasi has left an indelible mark on the game.

Throughout her career, Taurasi has accumulated an impressive list of accomplishments. She has led her team to three WNBA championships and has been recognized with numerous individual awards, including WNBA Finals MVP, WNBA Most Valuable Player, and WNBA All-Star Game MVP. Her ability to consistently perform at a high level has solidified her status as one of the greatest to ever play the game.

What sets Taurasi apart from her peers is her unmatched offensive prowess. She holds the record for the highest offensive win share in WNBA history, a testament to her scoring ability and impact on the game. Taurasi is a lethal outside shooter, capable of knocking down shots from anywhere on the court. But she is more than just a scorer; her basketball IQ and court vision make her an exceptional playmaker, constantly creating scoring opportunities for herself and her teammates.

But it’s not just Taurasi’s offensive skills that make her the greatest. She has a tenacious work ethic and a fiery competitiveness that fuels her performances. Whether it’s hitting a game-winning shot or making a crucial defensive play, Taurasi always steps up when it matters most. She has a remarkable ability to thrive under pressure and has earned a reputation as one of the most clutch players in WNBA history.

“Diana Taurasi is a once-in-a-generation talent. Her ability to score in a variety of ways and take over games is unmatched. She is the definition of a winner and a true champion.”

– WNBA analyst

Taurasi’s impact extends beyond the basketball court. She has been a role model for aspiring athletes, demonstrating the importance of hard work, dedication, and a never-give-up mentality. Her passion for the game and her relentless pursuit of excellence have inspired countless players and fans alike.

In conclusion, Diana Taurasi’s incredible skillset, numerous accolades, and undeniable impact on the game have solidified her position as the greatest WNBA player of all time. Her offensive dominance, clutch performances, and leadership qualities make her a player that will be remembered for generations to come.

Tamika Catchings – A Complete Basketball Player

Tamika Catchings, a forward for the Indiana Fever, is recognized as one of the most complete basketball players in WNBA history. She excelled in all aspects of the game, from scoring and rebounding to defense and leadership. With a championship, multiple MVP awards, and a relentless work ethic, Catchings solidifies her place among the best WNBA players of all time.

Throughout her career, Tamika Catchings proved to be a force to be reckoned with on the court. Her versatility and skill set made her a dominant player in every aspect of the game. Whether it was scoring clutch baskets, grabbing rebounds, or locking down opponents on defense, Catchings consistently displayed her talent and determination.

Known for her unwavering work ethic, Catchings approached each game with a fierce determination to win. Her leadership on and off the court inspired her teammates and earned her the respect of her peers. She was not only a superstar player but also a role model for aspiring basketball players everywhere.

“Tamika Catchings was the epitome of a complete basketball player. She could score from anywhere on the court, rebound with tenacity, and defend with a relentless energy. Her impact on the game went far beyond her statistics, as she was the heart and soul of the Indiana Fever for many years.”

Along with her individual accolades, Catchings led the Indiana Fever to a WNBA championship in 2012, solidifying her legacy as one of the greatest players in the league’s history. Her ability to elevate her performance in crucial moments and her determination to win set her apart from her peers.

In recognition of her outstanding contributions to the game, Tamika Catchings was inducted into the Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame in 2020. Her impact on the WNBA and her legacy as a complete basketball player will forever be remembered.

Cynthia Cooper – The Scoring Machine

Cynthia Cooper, a shooting guard for the Houston Comets, cemented her place among the all-time greats in the WNBA. Not only was she a key contributor to the team’s four championships, but she also revolutionized the game with her scoring prowess and innovative style.

A true scoring machine, Cooper had an uncanny ability to create scoring opportunities for herself and her teammates. She introduced the high-level pick-and-roll to the WNBA, a play that became a staple for many teams in the league. This offensive strategy allowed her to exploit defensive weaknesses and consistently put points on the board.

Cooper was known for her clutch performances, especially in the playoffs. When the game was on the line, she rose to the occasion, making big shots and leading her team to victory. Her ability to thrive under pressure earned her the nickname “Coop,” a testament to her leadership and mental toughness.

“I always wanted to be the player to take the last shot. I never shied away from it. I wanted to be the one to make the difference.”

Cynthia Cooper’s impact on the game extended beyond her scoring ability. She inspired a generation of young athletes, showing them what was possible through hard work and determination. Her legacy continues to influence the next wave of players who strive to follow in her footsteps.

In recognition of her incredible scoring ability, Cooper was inducted into the Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame, further solidifying her status as one of the best WNBA shooting guards of all time.

Maya Moore – The Face of the Lynx

Maya Moore, a forward for the Minnesota Lynx, has cemented herself as one of the best WNBA players of all time. As the number one draft pick in 2011, Moore immediately made her presence known in the league.

Throughout her career, Moore has displayed incredible versatility and all-around skills, solidifying her reputation as the best WNBA forward. She has an impressive list of accolades, including four championships, an MVP award, and multiple All-WNBA selections.

Moore’s impact goes beyond her on-court performance. She is the face of the franchise, representing the Minnesota Lynx with grace, passion, and a relentless work ethic. Her leadership both on and off the court has made her a role model for aspiring basketball players everywhere.

Maya Moore’s contributions to the game cannot be understated. Her skill set, championships, and leadership have established her as one of the greatest players in WNBA history. She embodies excellence and serves as an inspiration to future generations of basketball players.

Lisa Leslie – A Trailblazer in Women’s Basketball

Lisa Leslie, a center for the Los Angeles Sparks, is widely recognized as a trailblazer in women’s basketball. Her impact on the game, both on and off the court, has solidified her place among the best WNBA players of all time.

Leslie’s career was marked by numerous achievements and groundbreaking moments. In 2002, she became the first woman to dunk in a WNBA game, showcasing her exceptional athleticism and paving the way for future generations of female players.

“Being the first to dunk in a game was special, and it started a conversation about what women are capable of in basketball.”

Beyond her dunking ability, Leslie possessed a multifaceted game that made her a force to be reckoned with. As a dominant center, she excelled in scoring, rebounding, and shot-blocking, showcasing her versatility and impact on both ends of the court.

Throughout her career, Leslie accumulated an impressive list of accolades, including two WNBA championships, three MVP awards, and eight All-Star selections. Her on-court excellence and leadership elevated the Los Angeles Sparks to new heights and solidified her status as one of the best centers in WNBA history.

Off the Court Contributions

Beyond her athletic achievements, Leslie made significant contributions off the court as well. She played an instrumental role in popularizing women’s basketball and increasing its visibility and recognition. As a trailblazer for women’s sports, she shattered stereotypes and inspired countless young girls to pursue their basketball dreams.

Leslie’s impact extended beyond the WNBA, as she represented the United States in multiple Olympic Games, winning four gold medals with the national team. Her success at the international level further cemented her legacy as one of the greatest women’s basketball players of all time.

In addition to her athletic and cultural impact, Leslie has been an influential advocate for various social causes. She has used her platform and voice to champion gender equality, education, and breast cancer awareness, making a lasting impact on and off the court.

In conclusion, Lisa Leslie’s unparalleled achievements, groundbreaking moments, and off-court contributions have solidified her place among the greatest WNBA players of all time. Her iconic status as a trailblazer in women’s basketball will continue to inspire future generations and shape the future of the sport.

Sheryl Swoopes – The GOAT of the Comets

Sheryl Swoopes, a forward/guard for the Houston Comets, is widely regarded as one of the greatest all-around players in WNBA history. Her versatility on the court, combined with her impact on and off the game, solidify her place among the top WNBA players of all time.

With a career that spanned from 1997 to 2011, Swoopes left an indelible mark on the league. She was a key contributor to the Comets’ four consecutive championships from 1997 to 2000, showcasing her exceptional skills and leadership abilities.

“The opportunity to be coached by Sheryl Swoopes, to learn from her and compete against her on a daily basis, was invaluable. She set the standard for what it meant to be a true professional.” – Tina Thompson, former teammate

Swoopes’ impact on the game was not limited to her championship-winning years. She was a six-time WNBA All-Star, a three-time WNBA MVP, and a three-time Olympic gold medalist. Her ability to dominate on both ends of the court was a testament to her exceptional skills and basketball IQ.

Off the court, Swoopes paved the way for future generations of women’s basketball players. Her groundbreaking endorsement deals and her visibility as a high-profile athlete helped raise the profile of the WNBA and women’s sports as a whole.

Lauren Jackson – The Best from Australia

Lauren Jackson, an Australian center, is widely regarded as the best women’s basketball player from Australia. Her dominance and skill set make her one of the top WNBA players of all time.

Despite injury setbacks, Jackson left a lasting impact on the game. She showcased her talent with championship wins, MVP awards, and remarkable achievements throughout her career.

From her early years in the league, Jackson demonstrated her exceptional abilities on both ends of the court. Standing at 6’5″, she possessed a unique combination of size, athleticism, and basketball IQ, making her a formidable force in the paint.

Known for her versatile scoring ability, Jackson could dominate inside with her post moves and rebounding skills. She also had a deadly mid-range shot and extended her range to the three-point line, making her an offensive threat from all areas of the court.

Defensively, Jackson was a game-changer. Her shot-blocking prowess and defensive instincts disrupted opponents’ offenses and provided a strong presence in the paint. She consistently anchored her teams’ defense and was a reliable rim protector.

Off the court, Jackson’s impact extended beyond her on-court performances. Her passion for the game, dedication to her craft, and influential leadership inspired generations of players, both in Australia and internationally.
