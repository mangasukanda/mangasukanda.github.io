Jaylen Barron is an American actress who started her career with a few modeling assignments. She lived in Nevada till the age of 12 before she moved to Los Angeles to pursue her acting career. She has portrayed various important roles in popular TV and web shows like Shake It Up, See Dad Run, Shameless, and Free Rein. Jaylen has a huge social media fan base with more than 200k followers on Instagram.
