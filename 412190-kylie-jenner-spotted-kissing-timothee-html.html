Kylie Jenner Spotted Kissing Timothe Chalamet in Public Fan Claims She Could Be His Babysit

Publish date: 2024-05-20

Kylie Jenner, September 29, 2022. | Kylie Jenner and Timothée Chalamet, September 10, 2023. | Source: Getty Images

Cosmetics mogul Kylie Jenner and Academy Award nominee Timothée Chalamet have made their relationship official. Their recent public appearances mark the evolution of a romance that has been the subject of intense speculation since April, despite some social media comments playfully teasing an age difference between them.

Kylie Jenner and Timothée Chalamet have officially taken their romance public, sending the media and fans alike into a frenzy. The much-speculated couple, who've managed to keep their relationship tightly under wraps, showcased their affection at the Men's Singles Final at the US Open in New York, solidifying their status as Hollywood's newest power couple.

Since their covert beginnings, Jenner and Chalamet have kept their romance mostly hidden. However, as the saying goes, actions speak louder than words, and their recent public displays of affection have left fans both surprised and intrigued.

The duo's journey into the limelight began with whispers in April of a budding romance. Rumors swirled that the pair had been secretly dating since January, with eagle-eyed paparazzi spotting Jenner's Range Rover parked outside Chalamet's Beverly Hills residence. Notably, the two made their first low-key appearance together at Tito's Tacos in Santa Monica.

Kylie Jenner and Timothée Chalamet on September 10, 2023 in New York City. | Source: Getty Images

Insiders disclosed that their relationship, while exciting, was also refreshingly casual, a departure from Jenner's previous high-profile romances. Jenner's sister, Kendall, may have played a matchmaking role, making the transition into each other's lives that much smoother.

Fast forward to June, and we caught our first glimpse of the pair walking together in Beverly Hills, igniting curiosity and fueling rumors. However, amidst breakup speculations, sources close to the couple vehemently denied any such rift, reiterating that they remained an item.

Kylie Jenner and Timothée Chalamet on September 10, 2023 in New York City. | Source: Getty Images

Then came September 4, 2023, a date etched in the memories of fans worldwide, as Jenner and Chalamet chose Beyoncé's birthday concert to make their public debut. Surrounded by a star-studded audience that included Meghan Markle, Kelly Rowland, and Kerry Washington, the couple didn't hold back, cuddling and even sharing a passionate kiss.

In a viral video, they were seen cozying up on their private balcony, making it clear that they had officially stepped into the spotlight together.

Their love story continued to unfold just days later as they graced New York Fashion Week as a couple. Jenner accompanied Chalamet to a dinner celebrating Haider Ackermann's beauty collaboration with Augustinus Bader, setting the room abuzz with their presence.

Kylie Jenner and Timothée Chalamet at the 2023 US Open Tennis Championships on September 10, 2023 in New York City. | Source: Getty Images

Fan Claims Kylie Jenner Could Be Timothée Chalamet's "Babysitter"

Some social media users couldn't help but comment on the visual age difference between Jenner and Chalamet. It's worth noting that Chalamet is, in fact, older than Jenner, with the actor being 27 while Jenner is 26.
