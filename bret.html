Bret- Biography

Bret Michaels, an American rock singer, is best known as a member of the musical group Poison. Some of the rock band’s most well-known songs are Something to Believe In and Every Rose Has Its Thorn, to mention a few.

Bret Michaels found time to appear in reality shows like Rock of Love and his show, Bret Michaels: Life As I Know It, despite his successful music career with Poison. In addition, he was the winner of the third season of The Celebrity Apprentice.

Bret- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

Bret Michael Sychak was born on March 15, 1963. However, some people mistake him for Brett Michaels. His parents, Wally and Marjorie Sychak, greeted him in Butler, Pennsylvania. He is of English, Irish, Swedish, and German ancestry. Michelle Michaels and Nicole Michaels are his two siblings.

He grew up in Mechanicsburg and attended Mechanicsburg Area Senior High School. He was hospitalized to a hospital for three weeks when he was six, and he was later diagnosed with Type 1 diabetes. Micheals later underwent heart surgery in 2011. His passion for music began at an early age when he created a band called Paris.

Bret- Relationship, Girlfriend

Bret has never been married. But he was on the verge of becoming one. Well, he was engaged to his long-term girlfriend, Kristi Gibson.

In 1996, the Poison frontman began dating actress and model Kristi Gibson. As they became parents to a daughter called Raine Michaels, their bond blossomed (born in 2000).

Bret and his partner welcomed another daughter, Jorja Bleu Michaels, five years after welcoming Raine (born on 5 May 2005). While the family was expanding, Bret experienced a big setback when he suffered a cerebral hemorrhage and underwent an emergency appendectomy in early 2010.

Kristi, the singer’s baby mama, was by his side during this trying time. As a result, Bret wasted no time in closing the deal. On the reality show Bret Michaels: Life As I Know It on December 22, 2010, he proposed to Kristi.

Unfortunately, the couple’s wedding did not take place. Bret and Kristi were married for only two years before splitting up in 2012. Raine, their eldest daughter, is a rising model. She was one of the finalists for Sports Illustrated’s Swimsuit Edition in 2018, managed by One Model Management.

Jorja Bleu, Raine’s younger sister, is also a bright young lady. Unbroken is 2019 single written by her and her father, Bret.
