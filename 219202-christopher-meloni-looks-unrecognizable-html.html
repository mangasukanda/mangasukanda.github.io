When he is not working on getting better at carpentry, Meloni seeks out entertainment on social media. He once took to his Twitter page to share a shirtless photo of himself wearing a kilt and white socks.

Meloni is best known for his role in "Law & Order: Special Victims Unit," where he played "Detective Elliott Stabler" — partner to Olivia Benson (Mariska Hargitay) — for years before the crime drama's end in 2011.

Earlier this month, E! News revealed that Meloni would be dusting off his uniform to return to "Law & Order: Special Victims Unit" for the 22nd season premiere.

He will also reportedly appear in the spinoff of the drama series, "Law & Order: Organized Crime." In the spinoff, Meloni's character returns to the NYPD after a jarring personal loss.

The series will follow Elliott Stabler's journey to rebuild his life while leading a new and elite crew tasked with taking down the city's strongest crime syndicates one after the other.
