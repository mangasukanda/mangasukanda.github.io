Biography

Festus Ezeli was born in Benin City, Nigeria on October 21, 1989. Nigerian power forward/center who entered the NBA in 2012 with the Golden State Warriors. He helped the Warriors win an NBA Championship in 2015. In 2016, he signed with the Portland Trail Blazers. In 2012, he joined All-Star point guard Stephen Curry in Golden State. He played four years of college basketball at Vanderbilt, setting the school record for blocked shots in a season while earning second-team All-SEC honors in 2010-11. He was selected 30th overall by the Warriors in the 2012 NBA Draft.
