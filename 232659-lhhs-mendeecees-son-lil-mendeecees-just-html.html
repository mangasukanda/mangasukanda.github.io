L&amp;HH's Mendeecees' Son, Lil Mendeecees Turns 15 See Fans' Surprise by How Much He's Grown

Publish date: 2024-06-16

"Love & Hip Hop" star, Mendeecees Harris celebrated his son Mendeecees Jr.'s birthday with a sweet Instagram post and fans are in awe of the handsome young man he is growing up to be.

Mendeecees Harris recently celebrated his son Mendeecees Jr.'s birthday with a loving message on Instagram and while doing so, he treated his fans with a whole lot of photos and a video of him.

In the post, Harris wrote that he could not believe his son is 15 years old already. He was over the moon when he welcomed him into the world all those years ago, but it seems like yesterday that he came and changed his life forever.

Yandy Smith and Mendeecees Harris at the 2015 Dance Theatre Of Harlem Vision Gala. | Source: Getty Images

UNCONDITIONAL LOVE FOR HIS SON

Even though Mendeecees Jr. is his father's greatest weakness, at the same time, he is his inspiration who showed him the true meaning of unconditional and unequivocal love. Expressing his love for his son, the actor further wrote:

"You're truly amazing and a blessing to this world and I'm just proud to be your father..."

A SWEET PROMISE

The doting father ended his loving note with a promise that he will forever kiss his son until the day he dies and quipped that he will continue doing so even though he knows he hates it.

THE TEEN LOVES STAYING ACTIVE

The actor's sweet caption was touching indeed. He shared 9 snapshots and a video of his son and judging by those, it is not hard to tell that the 15-year-old has a keen interest in sports.

In one of the photos, a younger Mendeecees Jr. was seen wearing boxing gloves and a head guard. Three of the pictures were snapped after the young man enjoyed a game of basketball.

GROWING UP WELL

The teen is also passionate about fitness it seems. In a video, he effortlessly does pull-ups and dips while showing off his lean and muscular body. If that goes on, he will be ripped in no time. Meanwhile, fans could not believe how much the little one has grown up.

"Love & Hip Hop" fans know how his father and mother, Samantha Wallace, could not see eye to eye, but if there was one thing that they agreed on, it was their desire to give the best life to their little one and judging by the looks of it, they are doing a good job.
