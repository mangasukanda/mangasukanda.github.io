Tatiana Navka | Bio, Age, Net Worth, Relationship, Career

Tatiana Navka is a female figure skater from Ukraine. Her reign as the reigning world figure skating champion, which she won in 2004 and 2005, is her most renowned accomplishment. Furthermore, she had secured a gold medal during the 2006 Winter Olympics.

Additionally, with more than 1.2 million followers on Instagram, the figure skater has a sizable following.

Is Tatiana Navka Married? Relationship

Navka obtained dual citizenship in Russia and Belarus in 2002 and 1994, respectively. Previously, she resided in New Jersey.

In the year 2000, Navka wed Russian ice performer Alexander Zhulin. In May of 2000, Sasha, their daughter, was born in the United States. The divorce petition was initiated by the couple in the summer of 2009 and concluded in July 2010.

Nadezhda (Nadia), the daughter of Navka and Dmitry Peskov, the press spokesman for Vladimir Putin, was born in Russia in August 2014.

They were wed in a civil ceremony at a registry office in June 2015, and then in a more formal ceremony on August 1, 2015.

From 2014 to 2015, Navka received a benefit from Carina Global Assets Ltd., an offshore organization headquartered in the British Virgin Islands.

Navka may have underreported her income, proclaimed herself married for several years following her divorce from Zhulin, and lied to the IRS regarding the sale of a property, according to The Guardian.

In 2016, Navka and her dance partner, Andrei Burkovsky, provoked public indignation by donning the attire of Holocaust concentration camp detainees and competing in the Russian rendition of Dancing on Ice.
