Danielle Bisutti is an American actress, singer, songwriter, director, producer, voiceover artist, and writer who is best known for her portrayal of Amanda Cantwell in True Jackson, VP. Over the course of her career, she has also been cast in a variety of other shows and movies like Criminal Minds, Parks and Recreation, No Greater Love, The O.C., Two and a Half Men, Charmed, Wizards of Waverly Place, Last Man Standing, Curse of Chucky, and Dwight in Shining Armor.
