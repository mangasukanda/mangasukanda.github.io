Traci Braxton was an American singer, musician, reality television personality, actor, producer, and radio personality. Her discography consists of albums and tracks such as Crash & Burn, On Earth, Perfect Time, Lifeline, and Last Call. Traci had also been a part of shows and movies like Marriage Boot Camp, Braxton Family Values, Sinners Wanted, All In, and The Christmas Lottery.

    Traci Braxton Facts

  • She appeared as herself in the reality television series Braxton Family Values from 2011 to 2020.
  • On October 7, 2014, Traci Braxton’s debut studio album titled Crash & Burn was released and it included songs like Need It In My Life, Passion, Crash & Burn, Reasons, What About Love?, Holding On, and Last Call. The album peaked at #1 on the US Heatseekers Albums (Billboard) chart, at #18 on the US Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums (Billboard) chart, and at #108 on the US Billboard 200 chart. Also, it received mixed reviews and Tamar Braxton criticized the album for its use of autotune and tweeted: “Do the Grammys have a duet with auto tune category yet?”.
  • Traci Braxton was cast as Nana in the 2018 drama film Sinners Wanted.
  • Her 2nd and final studio album, On Earth, was released on August 24, 2018, via Soul World Entertainment and it consists of songs like Broken Things (ft. Toni Braxton, Towanda Braxton, and Trina Braxton), On Earth, Boy Bye, Out the Box, White Noise, and I Won’t Cry.
