Emma Booth is an Australian model turned actress who rose to fame for her appearance as Jill in the comedy-drama film Clubland that was internationally released as Introducing the Dwights in 2007. From there, Emma went on to portray various other well-known characters including Evelyn White in Hounds of Love, Molly McGee in The Gloaming, Kate Willis in Glitch, and Eloise Gardener in Once Upon a Time.
