Anthony Rapp is an American actor, voice actor, stage artist, author, and singer who is known for originating the role of Mark Cohen in the Broadway production of Rent. He has also been cast in many shows and movies including Star Trek: Discovery, Kidnapped, Rent, The Beach Boys: An American Family, Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, Dazed and Confused, Assault at West Point: The Court-Martial of Johnson Whittaker, 13 Reasons Why, The Good Fight, The Knick, Six Degrees of Separation, Adventures in Babysitting, A Beautiful Mind, Winter Passing, Let Them Chirp Awhile, and The Other Woman.
