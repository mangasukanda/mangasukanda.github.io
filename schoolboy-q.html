Biography

Schoolboy Q was born in Wiesbaden, Germany on October 26, 1986. Born Quincy Matthew Hanley, he is a hip hop recording artist and songwriter who became known for his work with the hip hop supergroup Black Hippy. He’s also popular for his independent album, Setbacks, which charted on the US Billboard 200. He collaborated with ASAP Rocky on “Brand New Guy” from 2011’s mixtape “Live. Love. ASAP.” He joined the 52 Hoover Crips when he was 12 and became a drug dealer primarily of Oxycontin, but also of crack and marijuana. He became serious about music when he was 21; he was also arrested and spent time in jail when he was 21.
