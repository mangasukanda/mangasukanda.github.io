Who is Ana Cobos?

A famous TV personality Ana Cobos(Anita Cobos) is a Spanish Sportscaster. She is known for her work in the Bein sports Network. Her known work in “The Express” gave her the popularity of her excellent brain to compliment her beauty.

Cobos has been working in the show since 2013 and mostly rule on the special previews and post-game coverage for the matches. Before joining the Bein network, she had experience in other Spanish sports channels like Marca TV, TVE, and Castilla-La Mancha.

Personal Life

The beautiful Ana Cobos was born on November 23, 1984, in Cordoba, Spain. Although she is famous as Ana, her given birth name is Anita Cobos Perez.

Early Life and Education

Ana was born and raised in Cordoba, Spain. She is a graduate from the University of Seville’s School of Communications with a Bachelor’s Degree in Journalism. Later she did a master’s in TV journalism from Rey Juan Carlos University. Initially, Cobos wanted to pursue her career in medicine in high school however she ended up choosing journalism as her career. She started her blooming career as a reporter and now she is a famous sports journalist.

Ana Cobos Career Journey

Cobos first worked in her professional field in 2005, as of the press office of the Sevilla Football Club. Her former work was as a reporter for TVE, Marca TV, and Castilla- La Mancha Television which is located in Spain.

Her first big work started in 2014. Ana also covered the “2014 FIFA World Cup”, held in brazil. Further, she has also worked with high-profile players like Rafael Nadal, Lebron James, and Lionel Messi.
