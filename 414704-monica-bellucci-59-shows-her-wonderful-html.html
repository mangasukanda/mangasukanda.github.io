Monica Bellucci, 59, Shows Her 'Wonderful' Boyfriend at Their First Red Carpet Appearance

Publish date: 2024-07-01
  • Monica Bellucci and her longtime husband, Vincent Cassel, parted ways ten years ago.
  • Bellucci recently confirmed that she was dating her new man, who is a film director.
  • The actress and her new boyfriend were spotted while out on their first-ever red carpet appearance.

Monica Bellucci walks the red carpet for "Ville-Marie" in Rome, Italy on October 20, 2015 | Source: Getty Images

Monica Bellucci is an exceptional actress known for movies such as "Malena" and "The Matrix Revolutions." She started her career as a model and eventually made her way to acting.

Bellucci has been married twice. The actress and her first husband, Claudio Carlos Basso, were married from 1990 to 1994. Bellucci then moved on with her second husband, Vincent Cassel. The two got married in 1999 and divorced in 2013.

Bellucci and Cassel met in 1996 on the set of "L'appartement," a French language film. They went on to date for three years before marrying in a low-key ceremony in Monaco on the French Riviera in 1999.

Monica Bellucci at home in Rome on June 6, 1991 | Source: Getty Images

The Italian-born actress and her second husband were blessed with two kids, Deva, 18, and Leonie, 13. The mom of two announced on August 26, 2013, that she and Cassel's marriage was over after 14 years.

On October 19, 2023, Bellucci and her boyfriend were seen in public for the first time ever since they confirmed their romance.

Vincent Cassel and Monica Bellucci at the 55th Cannes Film Festival Monica Bellucci in Cannes, France on May 24, 2002 | Source: Getty Images

Monica Bellucci and Vincent Cassel at The Third Rome Film Festival: Premiere of the Italian film "The Man Who Loves" in Rome, Italy on October 23, 2008 | Source: Getty Images

Bellucci, who was a Bond girl in the James Bond film "Spectre," is dating a new man ten years after her divorce from her second husband. The actress confirmed her new relationship after months of public speculation.

Bellucci and Tim Burton's Romance

Bellucci, who was born on September 30, 1964, is happy to have met her new man, Tim Burton. The actress spoke for the first time about her relationship with the director in an interview with Elle France. She confessed to loving Burton and having a great respect for him.

Rumors that the 59-year-old and Burton had developed a romance started when they were seen in October 2022 at the Lumiere Film Festival in Lyon. The pair's appearance in public came amid sources claiming that they had already started dating four months back.

In another interview with Harper's Bazaar, Bellucci gushed over her love for the film director. She said she found Burton to be a "wonderful spirit." The actress and the director first met in 2006 on the red carpet of the Cannes Film Festival, but they were both in relationships.

Now, Bellucci says she met a "spectacular soul." "The Apartment" actress not only adores Burton as her romantic partner but also praises his directing skills. The two are working on "Beetlejuice 2," a fantasy film that will be released in 2024.
