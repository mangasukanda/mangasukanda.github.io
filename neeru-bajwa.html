Neeru Bajwa is a Canadian actress, director, and producer who is best known for her work in several Punjabi and Hindi films like Jihne Mera Dil Luteya, Jatt & Juliet, Pinky Moge Wali, Special 26, Proper Patola, Laung Laachi, Munde U.K. De, Pata Nahi Rabb Kehdeyan Rangan Ch Raazi, Shava Ni Girdhari Lal, It Lives Inside, and Prince. She has also appeared on a variety of TV shows like Hari Mirchi Lal Mirchi, Astitva…Ek Prem Kahani, and Guns & Roses. She has also been featured in music videos for songs like Diljit Dosanjh‘s Raat Di Gedi and Guru Randhawa‘s Punjabiyan Di Dhee.

Boyfriend / Spouse

Neeru Bajwa has dated –

  • Amit Sadh (2004-2012) – Neeru Bajwa started dating actor Amit Sadh in 2004. They had met while working on the TV show, Guns and Roses and eventually fell in love with each other. They made their relationship public in 2005 by participating in the dance competition TV show, Nach Baliye. Their relationship was further thrust into the limelight as Amit was regularly seen pining for her during his appearance on the reality show, Bigg Boss. Soon, they were living together and it was even reported that they had got married. However, the marriage rumors proved to be false but they did get engaged. He even tried to help her gain a footing in Bollywood. However, it proved to be their undoing as they separated shortly afterward. They did manage to get back together but they didn’t last long as they separated in 2012.
  • Sajid Khan (2010) – In the summer of 2010, Neeru was reported to be dating Bollywood director Sajid Khan. It was reported that due to her relationship with Sajid, she had dumped Amit Sadh.
  • Harry Jawandha (2015-Present) – Neeru Bajwa got married to Harry Jawandha in February 2015. She gave birth to her daughter Aanaya Kaur Jawandha in 2015. Her pregnancy was written into the script of her movie, Channo Kamli Yaar Di. In 2020, they were blessed with twin girls.
