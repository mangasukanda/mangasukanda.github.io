Rebecca Kufrin is an American publicist and TV personality who became a household name in the United States in 2018 when she won the 22nd season of the dating and relationship reality TV series The Bachelor. Later that year, she was featured as the lead of the 14th season of The Bachelorette, a spin-off of the aforementioned hit TV show.
