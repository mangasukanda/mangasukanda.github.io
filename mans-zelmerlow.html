Måns Zelmerlöw is a Swedish pop singer, songwriter, model, actor, and television presenter. His discography comprises of albums and tracks like Barcelona Sessions, Heroes, Stand by For…, Christmas with Friends, Perfectly Damaged, Kära vinter, MZW, Time, Chameleon, Miss America, Work of Art (Da Vinci), December, Impossible, Run for Your Life, Should’ve Gone Home, Hanging on to Nothing, Alone on Christmas Eve, Mirror, Fire in the Rain, Glorious, Happyland, Brother Oh Brother, and Better Now. Moreover, he finished 5th in Idol 2005, won the 1st season of Let’s Dance, and won Melodifestivalen in 2015.
