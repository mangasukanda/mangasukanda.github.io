Biography

Robin Schulz was born in Murg, Germany on April 28, 1987. German DJ and remixer who has won worldwide recognition through multi-platinum remixes of tracks like Mr. Probz’ “Waves” and Lilly Wood and the Prick’s “Prayer in C.” His album Prayer was released in 2009 and cracked the top ten in three countries. He collaborated with singer Jasmine Thompson on the single “Sun Goes Down” in 2014. He began working as a DJ at age seventeen and first gained attention with his remixes posted on SoundCloud.
