Colleen Reed, from the third season of Love, is Blind, is a specialist in digital public relations.

Colleen was born in 1995 in Easton, Pennsylvania, and she is a dancer with Ballet North Texas in Dallas, Texas.

According to Cinemaholic, Colleen’s interest in ballet began at a young age when she began studying with Oleg Briansky and Mireille Briane. She subsequently studied with Karen Knerr at Pennsylvania Youth Ballet.

Colleen has danced at numerous ballets over the years, such as the Boston Ballet, the Orlando Ballet, and the Nashville Ballet.

In 2018, she graduated with a degree in Public Relations from the University of Oklahoma. The next year, Colleen completed her Bachelor of Fine Arts in Ballet Performance.

According to her Netflix profile, Colleen is excited to meet her soul mate and believes he is in the pods. Her longest romantic relationship lasted nearly five years. She also believes that when one discovers their soulmate, there is an instant connection.

“I am a firm believer that when you meet the ‘one,’ you will experience an immediate sensation, but I have not yet experienced this.”
