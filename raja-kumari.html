Biography

Raja Kumari was born in Claremont, CA on January 11, 1986. Songwriter and recording artist who has had a hand in the creation of many songs by popular artists, including Iggy Azalea‘s “Change Your Life” and Fifth Harmony’s “”Like Mariah.” She released her debut rap single, “Mute,” in 2016. She has been signed with Epic Records LA as well as Sony Music India. She co-wrote several songs from the Gwen Stefani album This Is What the Truth Feels Like. She began training in Indian classical dance at age 7. As a teenager, she became a respected freestyle artist performing under the alias Indian Princess.
