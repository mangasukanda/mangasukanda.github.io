31 Famous Voice Actor Celebrities and their Commercials

Publish date: 2024-05-28

Many of us have seen our favorite celebrities on TV commercials, advertising for various famous brands. From athletes to A-list movie stars, these celebrities are bringing brand recognition and trust to the products they advertise for.

In this article, we’ll take a look at some of the most popular celebrities that have lent their voices, faces and star power to different companies over the years.

John Krasinski – Esurance

John Krasinski, best known for playing Jim Halpert on NBC’s hit show The Office, lends his recognizable voice to Esurance’s TV commercials. Krasinski has also starred in several movies such as 13 Hours, Detroiters, and A Quiet Place.

His other voiceover work includes narrating a nature documentary called Earth: One Amazing Day and voicing a character in the 2016 Disney animated feature Zootopia.

Morgan Freeman – Visa

Legendary actor Morgan Freeman is the voice behind Visa’s TV commercials. Freeman, who has been acting for more than six decades, is one of the most recognizable faces in Hollywood.

He has starred in films such as The Shawshank Redemption and Driving Miss Daisy while lending his distinct voice to documentaries and cartoons like The Science Channel’s Through the Wormhole and The Powerpuff Girls Movie.

J.K. Simmons – M&Ms

Actor J.K. Simmons is known for his role as J Jonah Jameson in Sam Raimi’s Spider-Man trilogy, but he’s also the voice behind M&M’s commercials. He’s also known for his Farmer’s Insurance commercials as well.

Simmons is an award-winning actor with roles in films such as Juno, Whiplash, and The Closer, while also lending his voice to video games like Metal Gear Solid 4 and Marvel Ultimate Alliance 2.

Jason Sudeikis – Applebee’s

Actor Jason Sudeikis is the voice of Applebee’s TV commercials. He has had a long career in comedy, starring in films such as Hall Pass, Sleeping with Other People, and The Angry Birds Movie.

On television, he has starred in shows like Saturday Night Live and The Last Man on Earth. Sudeikis has provided voices for animated series such as Higglytown Heroes and Robot Chicken.

Willem Dafoe – Qwest

Actor Willem Dafoe is the voice behind Qwest’s ads. He has had a long career in Hollywood, starring in films such as Platoon, The Last Temptation of Christ, and Spider-Man. He also provided voices for two animated films: Fantastic Mr. Fox and The Boxtrolls.

Famous Faces and Names Behind Popular TV Commercials

Being a successful actor or actress that does commercial voice overs is no easy feat. It takes dedication and hard work to be successful in this field, but the rewards can be incredibly rewarding.
